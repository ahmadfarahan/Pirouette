Vira Talisa - Primavera

  • In stock

Description :

Internet phenomenon, at least on Soundcloud, made her breakthrough by releasing her first album in 2019. Vira's music cannot be separated from pop colors, especially those from France. The influence of French pop musicians from the 1960s such as Francoise Hardy, France Gall, to Serge Gainsbourg were some evidences in every Vira song. Which is not very surprising; considering that Vira lived in France for several years for study purposes. Vira Talisa invites us to celebrate a life full of delightful things through this album
